Welcome!

This is a site filled with odds and ends for players on Horse Phenomena! I'm Monstaccato (or Mango #1802 on HP), and I've been coding for around 13 years. I've been playing SIM games for over 15 and have been a part of the Horse Phenomena community for the past 5. In late 2018, I decided that I'd like to host a site to help players out! Included are point calculators, with training guides and more on the way!

The Baby Calculator

I created The Baby Calculator to help predict the points your baby will be born with. If you enter the exact points from both parents at the time of breeding, you'll receive the exact points of the baby, including awards given right after birth. You can also use the calculator to estimate the points the baby will be born with by entering the point goals of the parents. Check it out [ here ].
